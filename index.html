About Jinjigwan

Jinjigwan was founded to connect people through baking. By organizing bake sales, campaigns, and collaborative projects, we aim to strengthen bonds within our school and extend our impact to the local community.

Campaigns & Fundraising

  • On-campus bake sales and fundraising booths
  • Planning and operating nonprofit campaigns
  • Collaborations with external organizations
