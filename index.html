MISSION

Why we exist

Connection through food

Baking is a warm language anyone can understand. We use it to strengthen our school community—and extend support to the local community.

  • Connection — bringing people together
  • Service — sustainable giving & campaigns
  • Craft — quality, safety, responsibility
  • Transparency — clear reporting

What we promise

  • Before events: publish menu, prices, and donation goal
  • After events: share a simple cost breakdown and donation amount
  • Donation delivery: document with partner approval

* We design our process to align with school policies and partner guidelines.
